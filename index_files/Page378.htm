About Us

 

EMD Electronics is at the forefront when it comes to Professional Sound and Stage Lighting Technologies in the Philippines.

 

Started its operation in January 1982 and has earned the respect of discriminating clientele and professionals in the industry for our quality products and services.

 

EMD Electronics handles Design, Supply, Installation and Live Sound of Professional Sound and Stage Lighting Systems. The best minds in electronics engineering are behind the design of our products and systems. The company uses the latest equipment for design, production, installation and Live Sound to achieve perfect quality.

 

Our company often applies its expertise and resources in custom design and manufacturing of audio and lighting products as well as setting up systems for large venue indoor and outdoor events.  Customers come to us with their special needs at different stages of product development and system requirement.  For us, quality is of utmost importance, thus we have implemented strict quality control measures to meet industry standards.

 

For us, we do not just provide you products; we provide you solutions and quality service.
